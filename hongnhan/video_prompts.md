## HƯỚNG DẪN SỬ DỤNG PROMPTS

### Cho AI Video Generation (Runway, Pika, Sora):

- Thêm motion: `smooth camera movement`, `slow dolly in`, `parallax effect`
- Duration: specify exact seconds needed
- Style: `cinematic 4K`, `travel documentary`, `lifestyle vlog`

### Cho Video Production Brief:

- Shot list với duration mỗi cảnh
- Camera equipment: drone, gimbal, underwater housing
- Audio requirements: natural sound, music genre, voiceover

### Cho Social Media:

- Optimize aspect ratio: 9:16 (Reels/TikTok), 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Feed)
- Add captions/subtitles cho accessibility
- Hook trong 3 giây đầu
