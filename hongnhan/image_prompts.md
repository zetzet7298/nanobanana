- Thêm style: `--style raw`, `--v 6`, hoặc `photorealistic, 8K, HDR`
- Aspect ratio: `--ar 16:9` cho landscape, `--ar 9:16` cho mobile/stories
- Thêm lighting: `golden hour lighting`, `soft natural light`, `dramatic sunset`

### Cho Stock Photo Search:

- Sử dụng keywords chính: Binh Hung island, Vietnam beach, tropical paradise, seafood, coral reef
- Filter: tropical, island, beach resort, adventure travel, food photography

### Cho Photographer Brief:

- Sử dụng prompts làm shot list chi tiết
